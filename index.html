Thoughts on Progrssive Enhancement

Progressive Enahncement is a website design philosophy that focuses on building a foundational layer of content. Then adding another more advanced layer with all the bells and whistles later.

With progressive enhancement I like that the focus is planning out your website in advance and getting the basics done first. It allows you to do fixes and relatively easy instead of trying to figure out a site design baseed off of Graceful Degradation.

A big feature of PE philosophy is the approach towards older technology or browsers. When designing websites through PE the other focus is for the content to be accessible across many browsers and meant to work with a usuer's older technology.

I believe Progressive Enhancement is important because through its design the content will reach the biggest number of people, whether you still have an Iphone 4, need a screen reader, or dont have the fastest wifi speed PE is built to accomadate all of those problems.
